Why Mi Casita

The household runs smoother when everyone can see it.

No more "wait, who's picking up whom?" texts. Everything a family tracks lives in one shared home, current for everyone.

One shared calendar

Practices, appointments, and pickups color-coded by who they belong to — always current, on everyone's screen.

To-dos & chores that stick

Assign it, check it off, and see what's still open before it turns into a Sunday-night fire drill.

A grocery list that's at the store

Add from anywhere; whoever's out grabs it. The list is finally in the same place as the person shopping.

Budget & goals, no spreadsheet

Track the household budget with a running balance, and save toward what's next — together, in plain numbers.
